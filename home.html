Welcome to Carlsberg Vietnam

Carlsberg entered Vietnam in 1993 through a joint venture company.

Carlsberg Vietnam produces 1664 Blanc, Carlsberg, Tuborg, Huda, Huda Ice Blast, Huda Gold, and Halida.

Carlsberg Vietnam has 1 brewery in Hue.
